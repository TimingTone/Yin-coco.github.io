How to become a CHAMPION
Created 2019-10-06| Updated 2019-10-06|White Moon
Word count: 141|Reading time: 1 min|Post View:

In front of me is the Mr.Universe title.

So, every rep that I do gets me close to accomplishing the goal, to make this goal, this vision turn into reality.

Every single set that I do, Every repetition, every weight hat I list, gets me a step closer to turn this goal into reality.

So, I couldn’t wait to do another 2000 reps of sit-ups; I couldn’t wait for the next exercise.

For me, at the age of 20, I went to London and won the Mr.Universe condus as youngest Mr.Universe ever.

And it was because I had a goal.

So let me tell you that visualizing your goal and going after it makes if fun.

You got to have a purpose no matter what you do in life, you got to have a purpose.
